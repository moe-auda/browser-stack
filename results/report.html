Chart Notes

iPhone 12 (iOS) — Speed Lab captures FCP and Page Load Time only. Speed Index, LCP, TBT, and TTI are not available on iOS. These are excluded from those charts automatically.

OS X Big Sur — Safari — Speed Lab uses WebPageTest for Safari, capturing FCP, Page Load Time, and Speed Index. Lighthouse CWV metrics (LCP, TBT, TTI) are Chrome-only and excluded from those charts automatically.

Samsung Galaxy S10 — Full Lighthouse metrics (FCP, LCP, TBT, TTI, Speed Index). Page Load Time is not captured on Android.
